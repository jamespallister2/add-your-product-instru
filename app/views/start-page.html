Add your product to the service catalogue

The NHS England digital service catalogue is intended to be a single source of truth about NHS England's national digital services and programmes.

It hosts up-to-date information on digital products to make it easier for internal and external teams to integrate with and use them.

Use this guidance to help you:

  • add a new product or service to the NHS service catalogue
  • update an existing page

Before you start

The service catalogue is for services or programmes that are:

  • national in scale
  • owned by NHS England
  • something to do with digital capability and/or digital transformation

    • To complete this process you will need to have the following:

  • a good understanding of what your service does
  • information that allows you to draft a service page
  • time with the product owner or whomever can say the information is correct
